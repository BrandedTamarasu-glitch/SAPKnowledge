The 12 patterns (from RESEARCH.md):

1. **Make-to-Stock Production with Standard Costing** (MM, CO) — Standard cost estimate CK11N/CK24, production order settlement CO88, variance analysis
2. **Engineer-to-Order with Project Cost Collection** (MM, CO) — Internal orders or WBS for cost collection, settlement to asset or cost center
3. **Consignment Stock Management** (MM, SD, FI) — Vendor consignment (101K/201K) and customer consignment (631-634), VKOA billing at issue
4. **Third-Party Drop Shipment** (MM, SD, FI) — TAS item category, auto-PR, no inventory, two independent invoices
5. **Subcontracting (Toll Processing)** (MM, FI) — 541 component transfer, 543 consumption, BOM-based, special procurement key 30
6. **Intercompany Sales / Stock Transfer** (MM, SD, FI) — Cross-CC STO, intercompany billing IV/PI, VKOA for IC revenue
7. **Overhead Allocation to Products** (CO) — Assessment/distribution cycles, overhead costing sheet, KGI2
8. **Investment Project Capitalization** (CO, FI) — Internal order type 01 (investment), AIAB settlement to asset, AUC flow
9. **Batch-Managed Inventory with Shelf Life** (MM, SD) — OISO activation, CT04 characteristics, shelf life MHDRST, SD batch split
10. **Serial Number Tracking to Asset Register** (MM, FI) — MARC-SERAIL profile, IE01 equipment master, asset sub-number
11. **Split Valuation for Multiple Stock Categories** (MM, FI) — OMWC activation, valuation types, separate OBYC stock accounts
12. **Customer Returns and Credit Processing** (MM, SD, FI) — RE order type, 651 returns GR, credit memo VF01 RE

CRITICAL guidelines per RESEARCH.md:
- Patterns must start with a BUSINESS requirement (not a config task) and span at least 2 modules

- Each pattern's "See also" links to playbooks.md for scenarios that have playbooks, and to module decision trees for config choices
